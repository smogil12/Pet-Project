A Shared Passion

While attending Columbia University, School of Engineering, four full-stack developers discoveredÂ they had a shared passion ... DOGS! From that moment, Spencer Mogil, Ben Florentino, John Tuhaise and Liz Levine made it their mission to combine their collective "chops" and create an organization that would match owners and families with appropriate dogs.


The mission of Spot On Rescue came about from a little known fact, more than half of the 3.3 million homeless dogs were "given up" by owners who were unaware of the level of activity and socialization required of some breeds. Spot On is committed to setting you up for success from the very start! Thank you for loving dogs as much as we do!
