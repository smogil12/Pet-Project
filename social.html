Our Mission, Vision and Values

Welcome to Spot On Dog Rescue - the spot where dogs (and humans)in need of companionship get matched up according to personality and lifestyle.  We provide you with real-time feedback as to which adoptable dog are best suited to your needs.


The Mission of Spot On Dog Rescue is to bring about a time when there are no more homeless dogs.  We do this by building strong community programs and partnerships all across the nation. We believe that by working together we can save them all.

Our Vision and Values: to build a better world through love and kindness to all animals.

To get started, just click on the About You tab at the top of this page and you'll be directed to a brief questionnair about your preferences. Based on your responses and locations of available dogs, we'll provide you with a list of eligible pups near you. That's it... happy hunting!
